## Algorithms and Logic

The automated onboarding workflow begins when a new client form or CRM record is submitted with key details like brand name, account manager, start date, and billing contact. The agent then sends a personalized welcome email, creates a structured Google Drive folder with the correct permissions, generates a Notion client hub from the latest template, and adds a fully populated record in Airtable. After completing these steps, it sends a summary with all links to the account manager and logs the onboarding with timestamps and any errors, ensuring a consistent, reliable, and fully automated process.
